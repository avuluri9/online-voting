Why this website?....

This website is designed to bring about several advantages, such as security, convenience, accuracy, transparency, and accessibility, which can significantly improve the voting process and encourage higher voter participation.

Traditional voting methods often require voters to physically visit a polling station, which can be time-consuming and inconvenient, especially for those with busy schedules, physical disabilities, or those living in remote areas. Online Voting Systems enable voters to participate from the comfort of their homes or any location with internet access, making it convenient and accessible.
